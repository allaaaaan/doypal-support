# Privacy Policy — Doy Pal

**Last updated:** March 3, 2026

## Overview

Doy Pal is a mission and reward tracker app for parents and kids. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Doy Pal does not:

- Collect personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Use analytics or crash reporting services
- Connect to the internet
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies or similar technologies

## Data Storage

All data (missions, rewards, templates, and redemptions) is stored **locally on your device only** using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to any server or cloud service.

## Photos

If you add photos to rewards, those images are stored locally on your device within the app's data. They are never uploaded or shared.

## Children's Privacy

Doy Pal is designed to be operated by a parent on behalf of their child. The app does not collect any information from children. All data remains on the parent's device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete any data within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted here with an updated date.
